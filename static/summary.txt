 NHS is revising booking system for Covid-19 jabs in England after complaints. NHS Digital said system had no direct access to medical records and allowed millions to quickly book jabs. Big Brother Watch called it a "shocking failure"  NHS Digital said it was revising the messages used by the booking system to improve privacy. But it suggested that conclusions about someone's vaccine status from entering other people's data would not always be accurate. Germany opposes waiving Covid vaccine patents, saying patents are not hindering production. England's vaccine rollout extends to all over-40s.
